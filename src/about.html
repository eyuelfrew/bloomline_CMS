About us

BLOOMLINE Consulting and Trading PLC is a dynamic and innovative construction management consulting company that was founded in August 2023. With a commitment to excellence, we provide a wide range of services in construction supervision and management, contract management, Construction project claim analysis, risk management, training, project planning and evaluation, strategic plan development, Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) document preparation and design review and adaption.
